This Terraria mod is about the game Changed by DragonSnow. It adds laboratories on the surface where you will find Latex beasts. They are quite strong and grow stronger with every boss you defeat.

-------- Contents ---------

- 5 biomes
- 3 Town NPCs
- 22 normal monsters
- Some extra monsters on the drunk world
- 3 Bosses
- 5 upgradeable weapons
- 3 mounts
- Many furniture items
- Transformation mechanic (WIP)


------- Mod support -------

This mod has built-in support for:
- Biome Titles
- Boss Checklist
- Census
- Fargos Mutant Mod
- Music Display
- Wikithis

Additionally, town npcs can sell fruits and herbs from these mods:
- Calamity
- Spirit and Spirit Reforged
- Thorium

Mods that can cause issues:
- Modliquid library: there is a bug in this mod that causes waterfalls to freeze in all modded biomes, included the Changed biomes.
- MrPlague's Authentic Races: Not game-breaking, but the visuals of races are overwritten when transfurred.

Most other mods should work fine. The only problem that you can run into is when the world generation overwrites structures or biomes from other mods and vice versa.


------- Multiplayer -------

Most things should work fine in multiplayer. In rare cases, you might encounter desycing of player transfurs. This means that they are transfurred, but you can't see it. A possible indicator of this happening is if the other player jitters on your screen when he moves. To fix this problem, he has to transfur into something else with any of the combined syringes (it's hard to explain, but it has to be a syringe that makes the interface pop up).


---------- Other ----------

Most items and NPCs used to have scaled down sprites from Changed as placeholders. A lot of them have since been updated, but not all of them. You may come accross things that clash with Terraria's art style.

--------- Changelog ---------

This update adds achievements and fixes some issues with shimmer.
For information about previous versions, please check out the change notes in the steam workshop.


0.5.5

General
- Added support for Biome Titles
- Removed latex beast shimmer transmutations, because they were buggy. Now they just become transparent.
- Greatly optimized the code for the dryad's world status. Now the game won't hang anymore when you click the button.

Snow
- Latex can spread through ice like the evils biomes and hallow. It can also spread through snow.
- Added Glacial Goo, a slime variant for ice
- It makes you slip endlessly like the slippery blocks in Changed
- If you want the Changed experience, there is a config option to enable the slipping sound. It is disabled by default.

Achievements
- Let there be Light: Turn on the power in a laboratory.
- Rocket Science: Make a gas cylinder fly away.
- Citrus Shrine: Find a citrus shrine underground.
- Cuddly Colossus: Defeat a very large latex.
- Gooey Expedition: Find all biomes from this mod.

Fixes
- Removed broken iris scanner interaction text.
- Some blocks had the wrong hit sound.
- Wikithis not always directing to the right pages.
- Prototype did not become transparent from shimmer.
- Hats of shimmering latex beasts did not become transparent.
- It was possible that the strange swivel chair would not summon the wolf king if the black goo room had the smallest possible room height of 9 blocks.
- Hungry locker grew bigger from evolution
