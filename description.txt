This Terraria mod is about the game Changed by DragonSnow. It adds laboratories on the surface where you will find Latex beasts. They are quite strong and grow stronger with every boss you defeat.

-------- Contents ---------

- 3 surface biomes
- 3 Town NPCs
- 22 normal monsters
- Some extra monsters on the drunk world
- 3 Bosses
- 5 upgradeable weapons
- 2 mounts
- Many furniture items
- Transformation mechanic (WIP)


------- Mod support -------

This mod has built-in support for:
- Boss Checklist
- Census
- Fargos Mutant Mod
- Music Display
- Boulder Backport (needs to be installed if you want to see dialogue portraits)

Additionally, town npcs can sell fruits and herbs from these mods:
- Calamity
- Spirit and Spirit Reforged
- Thorium

It should also work fine with most other mods. The only issue that can occur is when the world generation overwrites structures or biomes from other mods and vice versa.
Please note that Boulder Backport can crash your game when reloading mods. This has nothing to do with this mod.
